What is VEGINDER?

Veginder is a service for finding shops and cafes with eco products, as well as the ability to place an order and track its status.

We believe that being eco-friendly is easy and inexpensive, and we also wish to provide a new perspective on the development of delivery services.

Organization culture:

— Honesty and openness

— Respect and tolerance

— Unity

— Responsibility and assistance

— Versatile focused on attracting resources

With this service you will be able to explore various shops and restaurants, as well as order groceries and other products.

You can also register your own establishment and place its products.
